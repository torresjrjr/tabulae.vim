*tabulae.txt* - A Vim spreadsheet calculator plugin
Last Changed: 2019 Dec 17

TABULAE.VIM                                                *tabulae* *tabulae-vim*

This plugin lets you open, edit, and export spreadsheets with the power of Vim.
tabulae.vim works with it's own filetype - .tae - which is a tab-delimited
spreadsheet with metadata.
